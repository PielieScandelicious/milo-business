### by DeepmAInd B.V.

## The Problem

Belgian consumers have no idea where their grocery money actually goes.
Their bank app shows "€147 at Colruyt" — not what they bought.
And brands spend hundreds of thousands on market research data that's slow, survey-based, and limited to one retailer.

---

## The Solution

**Milo is a Belgian iOS app that scans supermarket receipts and turns every shopping trip into real financial and health insights.**

Users point their phone at a receipt. Within seconds, every product is extracted, categorized, health-scored, and added to their spending dashboard — automatically.

---

## What Users Get

| Feature | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Receipt Scanning** | AI reads every item on your receipt instantly — across all 16 Belgian chains |
| **Spending Dashboard** | See exactly where your money goes: by store, category, week, month, year |
| **Health Score** | Every food item gets a 0–5 nutritional score. See how healthy your shopping really is |
| **AI Chat (Milo)** | Ask "Am I spending more on snacks this month?" and get a real answer |
| **Budget Tracking** | Set monthly limits by category. Get alerts before you overspend |
| **Deal Recommendations** | Personalized promotions based on what you actually buy — not random coupons |
| **Expense Splitting** | Split any receipt with friends or roommates, down to the individual item |
| **Rewards & Badges** | Earn coins, unlock tiers, and redeem real coupons for staying consistent |

**Pricing:** €5.99/mo (25 receipts) → €17.49/mo (150 receipts) | 14-day free trial

---

## What Brands Get — The Data Panel

Milo transforms anonymized receipt data into a **B2B consumer panel** — the same type of data Nielsen and GfK sell, but better:

| Traditional Panel Data | Milo Panel Data |
|---|---|
| Survey-based (memory bias) | Real receipts (actual purchases) |
| One retailer (loyalty card) | All 16 Belgian chains |
| Slow (monthly/quarterly) | Near real-time (daily ingestion) |
| Expensive (€100K+/year) | Fraction of the price |

**What brands receive each month:**

- **SKU Performance Report:** For every product — how many households bought it, how often, at what price, during which promotions, in which stores, vs. which competitors
- **Brand Switching Report:** Who's loyal to your brand, who switched away, where they went, whether promos actually retained customers

**B2B Pricing:** €2,500–€50,000 per report | Custom research from €5,000
