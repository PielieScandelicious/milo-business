        \begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=1.5em, itemsep=8pt]
            \item \textbf{No direct competitor exists} in the health + receipt + analytics intersection

            \item \textbf{Receipt rewards apps} (Fetch, Ibotta) have user bases familiar with scanning but different value propositions --- MILO targets users wanting insights, not points

            \item \textbf{Finance apps} (Copilot, Monarch) offer analytics but lack receipt/item-level data and health features --- MILO is complementary or superior for receipt-based tracking

            \item \textbf{Health apps} (Yuka) have ideal user demographics but no finance features --- potential partnership or user acquisition source

            \item \textbf{Pricing is aggressive} --- \$2/mo entry undercuts all premium competitors while maintaining full feature access

            \item \textbf{Defensive moat} is the unique combination of AI-powered receipt scanning, health scoring, and spending analytics
        \end{enumerate}
