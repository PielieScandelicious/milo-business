# 📡 Channel Strategy
## Multi-Channel Marketing Approach

---

## 🎯 Channel Strategy Overview

MILO's channel strategy follows a **digital-first, mobile-native** approach aligned with our target audience's media consumption habits and our lean marketing budget.

### Strategic Principles

1. **Owned Before Paid**: Build content assets before scaling paid
2. **Mobile-First**: All channels optimized for mobile experience
3. **Community-Driven**: Leverage influencers and advocates over mass media
4. **Data-Driven Optimization**: Continuous testing and iteration
5. **Integrated Experience**: Consistent messaging across all touchpoints

## 📈 Channel Performance Framework

### Attribution Model
- **Primary:** Last-touch attribution (for simplicity at launch)
- **Secondary:** Multi-touch attribution (scale phase)
- **Tools:** Adjust, AppsFlyer, or Branch for mobile attribution

### Channel Scorecards

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| **CAC** | Cost to acquire one user | <$15 |
| **Conversion Rate** | Installs → Active users | >60% |
| **LTV:CAC** | Customer lifetime value ratio | >3:1 |
| **Payback Period** | Months to recover CAC | <6 months |
| **Organic Ratio** | Organic / (Organic + Paid) | >40% at scale |

### Channel Testing Protocol

1. **Minimum Viable Test:** $500-$1,000 per channel
2. **Success Threshold:** CAC within 150% of target
3. **Scale Decision:** After 100+ conversions with statistical significance
4. **Kill Criteria:** CAC >2x target after optimization attempts
