# 📊 Metrics & KPIs
## Performance Measurement Framework

---

## 🎯 Metrics Overview

Effective measurement is critical to MILO's marketing success. This framework defines what we measure, how we measure it, and what targets we're aiming for.

### Measurement Philosophy

1. **Measure what matters** — Focus on metrics that drive business outcomes
2. **Leading + Lagging** — Track both predictive and outcome metrics
3. **Actionable** — Every metric should inform a decision
4. **Accessible** — Dashboards available to all stakeholders
5. **Honest** — Include metrics that might show problems

---

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### North Star Metric

**Monthly Active Users (MAU)** with at least one receipt scanned

*Why:* This metric captures both acquisition AND activation—the true measure of valuable users.

### Primary KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Why It Matters |
|-----|------------|----------------|
| **MAU** | Users active in last 30 days | Core growth metric |
| **CAC** | Total marketing spend / new users | Efficiency measure |
| **LTV** | Lifetime revenue per user | Unit economics |
| **LTV:CAC Ratio** | LTV divided by CAC | Sustainable growth |
| **Paid Conversion** | % of users upgrading to Gold/Platinum | Revenue health |
| **Retention (D30)** | % of users active after 30 days | Product-market fit |

## 📉 Health Metrics (Warning Signs)

### Red Flag Thresholds

| Metric | Warning | Critical | Action |
|--------|---------|----------|--------|
| **D1 Retention** | <35% | <25% | Review onboarding |
| **CAC** | >$25 | >$35 | Pause paid, optimize |
| **App Rating** | <4.3 | <4.0 | Emergency response |
| **Churn (Paid)** | >7% | >10% | Retention intervention |
| **First Scan Rate** | <50% | <40% | UX investigation |

### Cohort Analysis Requirements

Track all metrics by:
- **Acquisition Source** (Organic, Paid, Influencer, etc.)
- **Time Period** (Weekly/Monthly cohorts)
- **Device/OS Version**
- **User Tier** (Silver, Gold, Platinum)
- **Geography** (if expanding)

## 🧪 Experimentation Framework

### A/B Testing Protocol

**Prioritization Matrix:**
| Factor | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Impact potential | 40% |
| Confidence level | 30% |
| Ease of implementation | 30% |

**Testing Requirements:**
- Minimum sample: 1,000 users per variant
- Statistical significance: 95%
- Minimum duration: 7 days
- Document hypothesis, results, and learnings

### Testing Roadmap by Quarter

**Q1:**
- Onboarding flow variants
- Pricing page layouts
- Ad creative themes
- Email subject lines

**Q2:**
- In-app upgrade prompts
- Feature discovery
- Landing page variants
- Influencer content types
