## 🏛️ Brand Foundation

## 🗣️ Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Attributes

| Attribute | What It Means | Example |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| **Empowering** | We enable, not lecture | "Here's what we found" not "You should..." |
| **Intelligent** | Smart, data-driven, precise | "Your produce spending is 23% above average" |
| **Approachable** | Friendly, not corporate | "Let's look at this together" |
| **Health-Positive** | Encouraging, not judgmental | "Great progress this week!" not "You ate too much junk" |
| **Trustworthy** | Transparent, honest | "Here's exactly how we use your data" |

### Tone Guidelines

#### ✅ DO:
- Use clear, simple language
- Be specific with data ("$47.32" not "about $50")
- Celebrate progress without being condescending
- Ask questions to understand user intent
- Be honest about limitations

#### ❌ DON'T:
- Use jargon or technical language
- Be preachy about health or spending
- Make users feel guilty about choices
- Overpromise on features
- Sound like a generic chatbot

### Example Voice Applications

**Push Notification:**
> 🛒 "Weekly spending summary ready! Your health score improved by 0.4 points. Tap to see what's working."

**Milo Chat Response:**
> "Looking at your last month, you spent $342 on groceries—about $50 less than your average. The biggest change? You cut back on beverages by 34%. Nice work!"

**Error Message:**
> "Hmm, we couldn't read that receipt clearly. Could you try taking another photo with better lighting? Pro tip: lay the receipt flat on a dark surface."

**Upgrade Prompt:**
> "You've scanned 10 receipts this month (nice dedication!). Upgrade to Gold to scan more and unlock detailed health trend analysis."

## 🎯 Brand Guidelines Summary

### Brand DO's
- ✅ Lead with health and wellness benefits
- ✅ Use specific data in messaging
- ✅ Maintain privacy-first positioning
- ✅ Be encouraging about user progress
- ✅ Differentiate from "just rewards" apps
- ✅ Emphasize AI intelligence and personalization

### Brand DON'Ts
- ❌ Never shame users for unhealthy choices
- ❌ Avoid generic budgeting language
- ❌ Don't compete on cashback/rewards
- ❌ Never minimize data privacy concerns
- ❌ Avoid overly technical or jargon-heavy copy
- ❌ Don't be preachy about health or spending
