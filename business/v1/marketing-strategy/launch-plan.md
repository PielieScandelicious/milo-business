## 🎯 Launch Overview

A successful launch is critical for MILO's momentum. This plan outlines the complete go-to-market execution from 8 weeks pre-launch through 12 weeks post-launch.

1. **500+ beta users** before public launch
2. **3,000 MAU** within first 90 days
3. **4.5+ App Store rating** within 30 days
4. **Media coverage** in 5+ relevant publications
5. **20+ influencer partnerships** activated at launch

## ⚠️ Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| **Server overload** | Medium | High | Load testing, auto-scaling |
| **App Store rejection** | Low | Critical | Submit early, follow guidelines |
| **Critical bugs** | Medium | High | Thorough QA, hotfix process |
| **API failures (Veryfi)** | Low | High | Error handling, fallbacks |

### Marketing Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| **Low press coverage** | Medium | Medium | Backup outreach, Product Hunt |
| **Influencer no-shows** | Low | Medium | Overbook by 20%, contracts |
| **Negative reviews** | Medium | High | Respond fast, fix issues |
| **Paid ads underperform** | Medium | Medium | Multiple creative variants |

### Contingency Plans

**If downloads below target:**
- Increase paid spend on best-performing channel
- Activate backup influencers
- Push Product Hunt launch
- Personal founder outreach

**If reviews are negative:**
- Immediate response and acknowledgment
- Rapid bug fixes
- Reach out to positive beta users for reviews
- Address issues publicly on social

**If technical issues:**
- Pause paid acquisition
- Communicate transparently
- Focus engineering on fixes
- Delay additional marketing push

## 🎯 Post-Launch Learning Agenda

### Week 1 Analysis

- Which channels drove most installs?
- What was organic vs. paid split?
- Which influencer content performed best?
- What features are users engaging with?
- What are the main complaints/issues?

### Month 1 Analysis

- Customer acquisition cost by channel
- Conversion rates through funnel
- Retention curves by cohort
- Feature usage patterns
- Revenue vs. projections

### Key Questions to Answer

1. Who are our best users? (Demographics, behaviors)
2. What drives conversion to paid?
3. What causes early churn?
4. Which channels are most efficient?
5. What content resonates most?
