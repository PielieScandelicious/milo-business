# 🌟 Influencer Marketing Strategy
## Partnerships, Creators & Community Building

---

## 🎯 Influencer Marketing Overview

Influencer marketing is MILO's **highest-trust acquisition channel**, leveraging authentic voices in health, wellness, and personal finance to reach our target audience. Research shows campaigns with micro-influencers deliver approximately **20:1 ROI** vs. 6:1 for celebrities.

### Strategic Objectives

1. **Partner with 50+ influencers** in Year 1
2. **Drive 20%+ of installs** from influencer-sourced traffic
3. **Build brand credibility** through authentic endorsements
4. **Generate UGC content** for paid amplification

## 🤝 Partnership Models

### Model 1: Product Seeding (Nano Influencers)
**Budget: $3,000-5,000 (Year 1)**

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Selection** | 30-50 nano influencers in target niches |
| **Compensation** | Free premium subscription (Platinum for 12 months) |
| **Expectation** | Authentic use, potential organic mention |
| **Tracking** | No affiliate code, brand mention monitoring |
| **Goal** | Build authentic user base, potential organic content |

### Model 2: Sponsored Content (Micro Influencers)
**Budget: $15,000-18,000 (Year 1)**

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Selection** | 15-20 micro influencers with strong engagement |
| **Compensation** | $500-1,500 per post + free premium |
| **Deliverables** | 1-2 posts/videos, authentic integration |
| **Tracking** | Unique promo code, affiliate link |
| **Goal** | Direct downloads, measurable performance |

**Deliverable Options:**
- Instagram Feed Post + Stories (3-5 slides)
- TikTok Video (60-90 seconds)
- YouTube Integration (2-3 minute segment)
- Instagram Reel (30-60 seconds)

### Model 3: Brand Ambassadors (Macro Influencers)
**Budget: $8,000-10,000 (Year 1)**

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Selection** | 3-5 macro influencers for deeper partnerships |
| **Compensation** | $2,000-5,000 per campaign + revenue share |
| **Deliverables** | Multi-post campaign, exclusivity in category |
| **Tracking** | Dedicated landing page, unique code |
| **Goal** | Brand awareness, credibility, tentpole content |

**Ambassador Benefits:**
- First access to new features
- Direct line to product team
- Co-creation opportunities
- Revenue share on referrals (optional)

### Model 4: Affiliate/Performance (All Tiers)
**Additional structure layered on sponsorships**

| Element | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Commission** | $2-5 per install, or % of subscription revenue |
| **Tracking** | Unique affiliate links via Impact/PartnerStack |
| **Payout** | Monthly, 30-day attribution window |
| **Bonus** | Volume bonuses for top performers |

### Performance Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| **Reach** | Total audience reached | Track by campaign |
| **Engagement** | Likes + comments / followers | >3% for micro |
| **Link Clicks** | Clicks on tracking link | Track conversion |
| **Installs** | Attributed app installs | Primary KPI |
| **CPI** | Cost per install | <$5 |
| **CAC** | Cost per paying customer | <$50 |
| **Content Quality** | Internal scoring (1-5) | >4 for reuse |

### Influencer Scorecard

| Score | Performance | Action |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| **A (90-100)** | Exceptional results, great content | Priority for future campaigns |
| **B (75-89)** | Good performance, meets expectations | Continue relationship |
| **C (60-74)** | Below average, issues identified | Review and feedback |
| **D (<60)** | Poor results or violations | Do not renew |

## 🔍 Influencer Discovery & Outreach

### Discovery Methods

| Method | Tools | Volume |
|--------|-------|--------|
| **Hashtag Research** | Instagram, TikTok native | Manual, high effort |
| **Platform Tools** | Creator marketplaces | Modash, Aspire, Upfluence |
| **Competitor Analysis** | Who are competitors using? | Manual monitoring |
| **Inbound** | Creators reaching out | Build submission form |
| **Referrals** | Existing partners recommend | Ask top performers |

### Vetting Criteria

| Criteria | Minimum Standard |
|----------|------------------|
| **Engagement Rate** | >2% (micro), >1% (macro) |
| **Audience Demographics** | 60%+ US, 60%+ target age |
| **Content Quality** | Professional, aligns with brand |
| **Brand Safety** | No controversial content |
| **Past Partnerships** | No competing apps recently |
| **Authenticity** | Real followers (audit tools) |

## ⚠️ Compliance & Legal

### FTC Compliance

All influencer content must:
- Include clear disclosure (#ad, #sponsored, Paid partnership tag)
- Be obvious to audience (not hidden in hashtags)
- Apply to all platforms and formats

### Contracts

All paid partnerships require:
- Written agreement (even for nano tier)
- Content approval rights
- Usage rights for repurposing
- Payment terms and timelines
- Termination clauses

### Content Restrictions

Influencers may NOT:
- Make health claims (weight loss, disease treatment)
- Guarantee financial outcomes
- Disparage competitors by name
- Misrepresent app features or pricing
- Use content without disclosure
