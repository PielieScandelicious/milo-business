## 🎯 ASO Overview

App Store Optimization is **critical for MILO's success**—70% of App Store visitors use search to discover apps, and 65% of downloads happen immediately after a search. ASO is our most cost-effective user acquisition channel.

### Strategic Objectives

1. **Rank Top 10** for 25+ priority keywords by Month 6
2. **Achieve 4.7+ star rating** within first 90 days
3. **Maintain 35%+ conversion rate** (impressions to installs)
4. **Drive 40%+ of installs** from organic App Store search

## ⭐ Ratings & Reviews Strategy

### Rating Optimization

**Target: 4.7+ stars within 90 days**

**Tactics:**

1. **Strategic Prompt Timing**
   - Trigger: After positive moments (health score improvement, helpful AI response)
   - Not after: First session, errors, negative experiences
   - Frequency: Max once per 14 days per user

2. **In-App Prompt Flow**
   ```
   "Are you enjoying MILO?"
        │
        ├─► Yes ──► "Would you mind rating us? It helps a lot!"
        │           ──► Native rating prompt
        │
        └─► Not really ──► "We'd love to hear how we can improve"
                          ──► Feedback form (not App Store)
   ```

3. **Post-Positive Moments**
   - After 3rd receipt scanned
   - After health score improves
   - After helpful Milo chat response
   - After upgrading to paid tier

### Review Generation

**Tactics:**

1. **Email Follow-ups**
   - Day 7 email to active users asking for review
   - Include direct link to App Store review

2. **Influencer Reviews**
   - Encourage honest reviews from partners
   - Disclosure required but authentic opinions

3. **Beta Testers**
   - Invite loyal beta testers to leave reviews at launch
   - These users are already advocates

### Review Response Strategy

| Review Type | Response Time | Approach |
|-------------|---------------|----------|
| 5-star | 24-48 hours | Thank, highlight feature they mentioned |
| 4-star | 24 hours | Thank, ask what would make it 5 stars |
| 3-star | 12 hours | Acknowledge, offer help, show improvement |
| 1-2 star | ASAP | Apologize, solve problem, invite to email support |

**Response Templates:**

**5-Star Response:**
```
Thank you so much, [Name]! We're thrilled MILO is helping you
understand your spending better. We're constantly improving
based on feedback from users like you. Stay tuned for exciting
new features! 🙏
```

**1-2 Star Response:**
```
Hi [Name], we're sorry MILO didn't meet your expectations.
We take all feedback seriously and would love to help resolve
your issue. Please email us at support@milo-app.com and we'll
make it right. Thank you for giving us the chance to improve.
```

## 🧪 A/B Testing Strategy

### Custom Product Pages (CPPs)

**Page Variants:**

| Page | Target Audience | Messaging Focus | Screenshots |
|------|-----------------|-----------------|-------------|
| **Default** | General | Full value proposition | All features |
| **Health** | Health-conscious | Health scores, nutrition | Health-focused |
| **Budget** | Budget-focused | Spending tracking, analytics | Finance-focused |
| **Mint** | Mint migrants | "Better than Mint" | Comparison |

### Test Priorities

**Phase 1 (Launch):**
1. Screenshot order (which feature first?)
2. Subtitle variations
3. Icon variations (if applicable)

**Phase 2 (Optimization):**
1. Custom Product Pages by audience
2. Preview video vs. no video
3. Screenshot background colors

### Testing Protocol

- Minimum test duration: 7 days
- Minimum traffic: 1,000 impressions per variant
- Success metric: Conversion rate (impression → install)
- Statistical significance: 95% confidence
