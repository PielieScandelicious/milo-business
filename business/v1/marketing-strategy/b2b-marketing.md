# 🏢 B2B Marketing Strategy
## Enterprise & Data Monetization Marketing

---

## 🎯 B2B Marketing Overview

B2B marketing drives MILO's **highest-margin revenue stream**, targeting enterprises seeking premium consumer data and insights. This strategy focuses on building credibility, generating qualified leads, and supporting enterprise sales cycles.

### Strategic Importance

| Metric | B2B Contribution |
|--------|------------------|
| **Revenue (Year 3)** | $6.5M (66% of total) |
| **Gross Margin** | 80-90% |
| **LTV:CAC** | 10-15:1 |
| **Strategic Value** | Category validation, exit optionality |

### Marketing Objectives

1. **Build pipeline of $500K+** in Year 1
2. **Secure 3-5 pilot agreements** with enterprise targets
3. **Establish thought leadership** in consumer data space
4. **Position for enterprise sales** starting Month 4

## 📈 Account-Based Marketing (ABM) Strategy

### ABM Program Structure

```
                    ┌─────────────────────┐
                    │   TIER 1 (1:1 ABM)  │
                    │   10-15 accounts    │
                    │   Custom campaigns  │
                    └─────────┬───────────┘
                              │
              ┌───────────────┴───────────────┐
              │      TIER 2 (1:Few ABM)       │
              │       25-50 accounts          │
              │    Segment-based programs     │
              └───────────────┬───────────────┘
                              │
        ┌─────────────────────┴─────────────────────┐
        │         TIER 3 (Programmatic ABM)         │
        │              100+ accounts                │
        │     Content marketing + paid media        │
        └───────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Tier 1 Tactics (1:1 ABM)

| Tactic | Description | Frequency |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| **Custom Research** | Bespoke data analysis for target account | Quarterly |
| **Executive Outreach** | Personalized founder/CEO outreach | Monthly |
| **Custom Content** | Account-specific whitepapers/presentations | Per account |
| **Event Hospitality** | Private dinners, conference meetings | Key events |
| **LinkedIn Engagement** | Consistent engagement with key contacts | Weekly |

**Example Tier 1 Campaign for Nestlé:**
1. Research: Analyze Nestlé's health-focused portfolio shifts
2. Custom insight: "How Health-Conscious Consumers Buy Nestlé Products"
3. Personalized outreach to VP Consumer Insights
4. Offer free pilot with health score data for their categories
5. Present findings, convert to paid agreement

### Tier 2 Tactics (1:Few ABM)

| Tactic | Description | Frequency |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| **Segment Webinars** | Industry-specific presentations | Monthly |
| **Targeted Ads** | LinkedIn ads to account lists | Ongoing |
| **Email Campaigns** | Segment-specific nurture sequences | Bi-weekly |
| **Custom Landing Pages** | Industry-specific value propositions | Per segment |

### Tier 3 Tactics (Programmatic)

| Tactic | Description | Frequency |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| **Content Syndication** | Reports distributed via industry channels | Quarterly |
| **LinkedIn Thought Leadership** | Regular publishing and engagement | 3x/week |
| **Retargeting** | Website visitors, content downloaders | Ongoing |
| **Inbound SEO** | B2B-focused content | 2x/month |

## 🗓️ B2B Marketing Timeline

### Phase 1: Foundation (M1-M3)
- Launch /for-business website section
- Publish first flagship report
- Begin LinkedIn thought leadership
- Identify and research Tier 1 accounts
- Attend 1-2 industry events

### Phase 2: Activation (M4-M6)
- Launch ABM campaigns for Tier 1
- Begin LinkedIn advertising
- Host first webinar series
- Develop first case study (pilot customer)
- Expand to Tier 2 accounts

### Phase 3: Scale (M7-M12)
- Full ABM program across tiers
- Regular webinar cadence
- Multiple case studies
- Partnership announcements
- Conference speaking engagements
