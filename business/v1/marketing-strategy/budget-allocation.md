# 💵 Marketing Budget Allocation
## Detailed Financial Planning & Resource Allocation

---

## 🎯 Budget Overview

MILO's Year 1 marketing budget is designed for **capital-efficient growth**, maximizing impact while proving unit economics before scaling.

### Total Marketing Budget: $125,000
*Source: 25% of $500K seed allocation for user acquisition*

### Budget Philosophy

1. **Test before scale** — Start small, prove ROI, then invest
2. **Diversify channels** — No single channel dependency
3. **Measure everything** — Attribution-driven spend
4. **Reserve for opportunity** — Keep 10% flexible
5. **Organic foundation** — Build for long-term efficiency

## 🔄 Budget Flexibility Rules

### Reallocation Triggers

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Channel CPI > 2x target for 2 weeks** | Reduce budget 50%, shift to winners |
| **Channel CPI < 0.5x target** | Increase budget 50% from underperformers |
| **Organic growing faster than expected** | Reduce paid, increase content investment |
| **B2B pipeline exceeds target** | Shift 10% from B2C to B2B |

### Reserve Usage

**$2,188 Testing Reserve** to be used for:
- New channel experiments (e.g., Reddit, Pinterest)
- Opportunistic campaigns (trending moments)
- Creative testing at scale
- Holiday campaign boosts

### Quarterly Rebalancing

- End of Q1: Major rebalance based on launch data
- End of Q2: Adjust for summer patterns
- End of Q3: Holiday planning allocation
- End of Q4: Year 2 budget planning

## ⚠️ Financial Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| **CAC exceeds projections** | Budget runs out faster | Weekly monitoring, quick pivots |
| **Channel underperformance** | Miss user targets | Diversified approach, testing reserve |
| **Delayed B2B revenue** | Cash flow pressure | Conservative B2B projections |
| **Apple Search Ads competition increases** | Higher CPIs | Diversify to TikTok, organic |
| **Influencer ROI disappoints** | Wasted spend | Performance-based compensation |
