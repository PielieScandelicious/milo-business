## 🎯 Strategic Overview

This comprehensive marketing strategy positions **MILO** as the definitive leader in wellness-aware spending analytics—a new category we are creating at the intersection of personal finance, receipt scanning, and health-conscious consumer behavior.

### Our Mission
To empower health-conscious consumers to understand not just *how much* they spend, but *whether they're spending it on healthy choices*—while building the most valuable, ethically-sourced consumer data platform in the market.
