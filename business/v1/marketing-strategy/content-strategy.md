### On-Page SEO Guidelines

**Title Tags:**
- Primary keyword at beginning
- Brand at end
- Under 60 characters
- Example: "Best Mint Alternatives 2026: Top Budget Apps Compared | MILO"

**Meta Descriptions:**
- Include primary and secondary keywords
- Clear value proposition
- Call-to-action
- 150-160 characters

**Header Structure:**
```
H1: Primary keyword + value statement
  H2: Major section (include keyword variation)
    H3: Subsection (long-tail keywords)
  H2: Another major section
    H3: Subsection
```

**Content Optimization:**
- 1,500-3,000 words for pillar content
- 1,000-1,500 words for standard blogs
- Keyword density: 1-2% naturally integrated
- Internal links: 3-5 per article
- External links: 2-3 authoritative sources

### Technical SEO Requirements

| Element | Requirement |
|---------|-------------|
| **Site Speed** | <3 seconds mobile load time |
| **Mobile** | Mobile-first responsive design |
| **Schema** | Article, FAQ, Organization markup |
| **Sitemap** | Auto-generated, submitted to GSC |
| **SSL** | HTTPS required |
| **Core Web Vitals** | All metrics in "good" range |

## 📊 Content Formats

### Blog Posts

**Standard Blog Post Template:**
1. **Hook** (50-100 words): Engaging opening, problem statement
2. **Context** (100-200 words): Why this matters
3. **Main Content** (800-1500 words): Actionable information
4. **MILO Integration** (100-200 words): How MILO helps
5. **CTA** (50 words): Clear next step

### Pillar Content

**Pillar Page Structure:**
- 3,000-5,000 words comprehensive coverage
- Table of contents with jump links
- Multiple H2/H3 sections
- Links to related cluster content
- Downloadable resources
- Video embeds where relevant

### Data Reports & Insights

**Quarterly Report Template:**
1. Executive Summary
2. Key Findings (3-5 data points)
3. Methodology
4. Detailed Analysis
5. Implications for Consumers/Industry
6. About MILO

### Video Content

| Type | Length | Purpose | Platform |
|------|--------|---------|----------|
| **Tutorials** | 2-5 min | Education | YouTube |
| **Feature Demos** | 30-60 sec | Conversion | App Store, Social |
| **Data Insights** | 60-90 sec | Authority | LinkedIn, Twitter |
| **User Stories** | 60-90 sec | Social Proof | Instagram, TikTok |

## 📱 Social Content Strategy

### Platform-Specific Content

#### Instagram
- **Feed**: Polished graphics, health tips, user milestones
- **Stories**: Quick tips, polls, behind-the-scenes
- **Reels**: 15-30 sec educational clips, trends

**Example Content:**
- Health score visualization graphics
- "Did you know?" nutrition facts
- User transformation stories
- Receipt scanning tutorials

#### TikTok
- **Style**: Native, casual, trend-aware
- **Content**: Educational shorts, trending sounds, reactions

**Example Content:**
- "POV: You check your health score"
- "Things I learned from my grocery receipts"
- Duets with health/finance creators
- "This or That" spending comparisons

#### Twitter/X
- **Style**: Conversational, data-driven, thought leadership
- **Content**: Insights, thread breakdowns, industry commentary

**Example Content:**
- Data insight threads
- Hot takes on health/spending trends
- Industry news commentary
- User question responses

#### LinkedIn
- **Style**: Professional, B2B-focused
- **Content**: Company updates, industry insights, thought leadership

**Example Content:**
- Consumer insights reports
- Industry trend analysis
- Company milestone announcements
- Team/culture content
