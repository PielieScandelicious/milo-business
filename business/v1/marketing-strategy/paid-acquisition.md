## 🎯 Paid Acquisition Overview

Paid acquisition serves as MILO's **growth accelerator**, driving rapid user growth while optimizing for quality and efficiency. Our approach balances aggressive growth with disciplined unit economics.

### Strategic Objectives

1. **Acquire 10,000 MAU by Month 12** with sustainable CAC
2. **Achieve CAC < $15** at scale (blended across channels)
3. **Maintain LTV:CAC > 3:1** for all paid channels
4. **Build reusable creative and targeting assets** for scale

## 🎵 TikTok Ads Strategy

### Why TikTok

- 44% of users are health-conscious millennials/Gen Z
- Lower CPMs than Meta in 2025-2026
- Strong organic discovery potential
- Native content performs better than polished ads

### Campaign Structure

| Campaign | Objective | Budget % | Creative Style |
|----------|-----------|----------|----------------|
| **TopView/Brand** | Awareness | 20% | High-production |
| **In-Feed (Spark)** | Traffic/Installs | 50% | Native/UGC-style |
| **In-Feed (Direct)** | App Installs | 30% | Direct response |

### Creative Best Practices

**TikTok-Native Principles:**
- Start with a hook in first 1-2 seconds
- Use trending sounds when relevant
- Show face/personality
- Less polished = more authentic
- Include text overlays (sound off)

**Content Formats:**
1. **"Day in my life"** with MILO integration
2. **"Things that just make sense"** health scores
3. **"POV"** style health score reveals
4. **Educational** quick health/finance tips
5. **Duets** with health influencer content

### Targeting

| Audience Type | Targeting |
|---------------|-----------|
| **Interest** | Health & fitness, personal finance, cooking |
| **Behavior** | App installers, engaged shoppers |
| **Creator** | Followers of health/finance creators |
| **Hashtag** | #healthyeating, #budgeting, #groceryhaul |

## 🧪 Testing Framework

### Creative Testing Protocol

**Phase 1: Concept Testing (Week 1-2)**
- Test 4-6 creative concepts
- $20-30 per concept
- Winner = lowest CPI or highest CTR

**Phase 2: Variation Testing (Week 3-4)**
- 3-4 variations of winning concept
- Test hooks, CTAs, formats
- $50-100 per variation

**Phase 3: Audience Testing (Week 5-6)**
- Test winning creative across audiences
- Identify best audience-creative pairs
- $100-200 per audience

**Phase 4: Scale (Week 7+)**
- Scale winning combinations
- Continuous refresh (new creative every 2 weeks)
- Monitor for fatigue

### A/B Testing Priorities

| Priority | Element | Test Approach |
|----------|---------|---------------|
| 1 | **Hook** (first 3 sec) | 4-5 variations |
| 2 | **Value Proposition** | Health vs. finance focus |
| 3 | **CTA** | "Download Free" vs. "Get Your Score" |
| 4 | **Format** | Video vs. static vs. carousel |
| 5 | **Length** | 15 sec vs. 30 sec vs. 60 sec |

## ⚠️ Risk Mitigation

### Common Risks & Responses

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| **CAC exceeds target** | Pause lowest performers, shift to higher-intent channels |
| **Creative fatigue** | Maintain 4-week creative refresh cycle |
| **Policy violations** | Pre-review all creative, avoid health claims |
| **Platform changes** | Diversify across 3+ channels |
| **Attribution issues** | Use multiple measurement approaches |

### Kill Criteria

Pause a channel/campaign if:
- CPI > 2x target for 7+ consecutive days
- Quality score drops significantly
- Fraud indicators detected
- LTV data shows poor user quality
