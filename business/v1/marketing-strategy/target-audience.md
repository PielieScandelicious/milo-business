# 👥 Target Audience Strategy
## Segmentation, Personas & Customer Journey

---

## 🎯 Audience Overview

MILO operates a dual-sided business model, requiring distinct audience strategies for:
1. **B2C Consumers** — Primary users driving data value
2. **B2B Enterprise** — Revenue through data monetization

---

## 📊 B2C Market Segmentation

### Primary Segments (High Priority)

#### Segment 1: Health Optimizers 🥗
**"I track what I eat, I should track what I spend on what I eat"**

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Size** | 22M+ in US (Yuka user overlap) |
| **Demographics** | 25-40, $60K-$150K income, urban/suburban |
| **Psychographics** | Health-conscious, data-driven, willing to pay for quality |
| **Behaviors** | Uses 3+ health apps, reads nutrition labels, organic preference |
| **Pain Points** | No way to track health impact of spending, scattered apps |
| **Value Proposition** | "See the health story behind every receipt" |
| **Acquisition Channels** | Influencer marketing, health communities, Yuka cross-promotion |

**Key Insight**: This segment overlaps heavily with Yuka's 22M US users. They already scan products—MILO extends this to their entire receipt.

---

#### Segment 2: Displaced Mint Users 💸
**"I need my budgeting tools back, but better"**

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Size** | ~6M actively seeking alternatives |
| **Demographics** | 28-45, $50K-$120K income, financially engaged |
| **Psychographics** | Budget-conscious, analytical, values automation |
| **Behaviors** | Previously used Mint daily, tracks spending categories, sets budgets |
| **Pain Points** | Lost their financial overview, alternatives lack receipt detail |
| **Value Proposition** | "Everything Mint was, plus item-level receipt insights" |
| **Acquisition Channels** | Reddit r/personalfinance, search ads, comparison content |

**Key Insight**: These users have proven willingness to engage with financial tools daily. They represent high-LTV potential.

---

### Secondary Segments (Medium Priority)

#### Segment 3: Wellness Families 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
**"I want my family to eat healthy without breaking the budget"**

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Size** | ~15M households |
| **Demographics** | 30-45, dual income, $80K-$180K, suburban |
| **Psychographics** | Family-focused, health-aware, value-conscious |
| **Behaviors** | Weekly grocery shopping, compares prices, meal planning |
| **Pain Points** | Balancing health with budget, tracking family spending |
| **Value Proposition** | "Know exactly what your family is eating and spending" |
| **Acquisition Channels** | Parenting influencers, Facebook groups, Pinterest |

---

#### Segment 4: Financial Optimizers 📈
**"I want complete visibility into every dollar"**

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Size** | ~10M users |
| **Demographics** | 25-50, $70K-$200K income, tech-savvy |
| **Psychographics** | Detail-oriented, spreadsheet lovers, FIRE enthusiasts |
| **Behaviors** | Multiple finance apps, exports data, creates custom reports |
| **Pain Points** | Bank transactions lack detail, can't see item-level data |
| **Value Proposition** | "Finally, spending data at the item level" |
| **Acquisition Channels** | FIRE communities, personal finance blogs, Reddit |

---

### Tertiary Segments (Lower Priority - Year 2)

#### Segment 5: GLP-1 Users 💊
**"I need to track what I'm eating more carefully now"**

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Size** | 15M+ current/potential users |
| **Demographics** | 35-60, variable income, health-motivated |
| **Psychographics** | Actively managing weight, new to detailed food tracking |
| **Behaviors** | Doctor-prescribed dietary changes, seeking tools |
| **Pain Points** | Need to monitor food choices more carefully |
| **Value Proposition** | "See your nutrition spending patterns at a glance" |
| **Acquisition Channels** | Health forums, GLP-1 communities, healthcare partnerships |

## 🎯 Targeting Strategy by Phase

### Phase 1: Launch (M1-M3)
**Focus: Health Optimizers + Displaced Mint Users**
- Narrow targeting for proof of concept
- Validate messaging with core segments
- Build testimonials and case studies

### Phase 2: Growth (M4-M6)
**Expand: Add Wellness Families**
- Broaden appeal with family messaging
- Launch influencer partnerships in parenting niche
- Begin B2B pilot outreach

### Phase 3: Scale (M7-M12)
**Full Expansion: All B2C + B2B**
- Mass market awareness campaigns
- B2B enterprise sales motion
- Referral and advocacy programs
