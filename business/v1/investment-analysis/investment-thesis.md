## The Core Investment Thesis

### <span style="color:#f59e0b">1. Category Creation Opportunity</span>

Milo is pioneering "wellness-aware spending analytics"—the first platform to combine receipt scanning with health scoring. This creates a new market category with defensible differentiation and premium pricing power.

### <span style="color:#f59e0b">2. Multi-Revenue Architecture</span>

Three distinct revenue streams (B2C subscriptions, B2B data monetization, analytics reports) provide diversification, reduce risk, and create multiple paths to value. Data monetization alone represents a <span style="color:#16a34a">**$6.5M opportunity**</span> at 100K users.

### <span style="color:#f59e0b">3. Exceptional Unit Economics</span>

LTV:CAC ratios of <span style="color:#16a34a">**3:1 to 15:1**</span> across segments, <span style="color:#16a34a">**71% operating margins**</span> at scale, and costs that decrease 35% per user as the platform scales. The economics support aggressive growth investment.

### <span style="color:#f59e0b">4. Privacy-First Competitive Moat</span>

Built from the ground up for CCPA/GDPR compliance with tiered consent, anonymization, and data governance. This enables premium B2B pricing (<span style="color:#16a34a">**$30-75/user**</span>) in an increasingly privacy-conscious market.

## Why Now? The Timing Advantage

### <span style="color:#f59e0b">1. Privacy Regulation Tailwinds</span>

CCPA, GDPR, and emerging state laws are creating demand for compliant data sources. Third-party cookies are dying. First-party, consented data commands premium pricing. Milo's privacy-first architecture is positioned perfectly.

### <span style="color:#16a34a">2. AI Cost Revolution</span>

The shift from Claude to Gemini 2.0 Flash reduced AI costs by <span style="color:#16a34a">**95%**</span>. Combined with mature OCR (Veryfi), the unit economics for AI-powered receipt processing are now viable at scale for the first time.

### <span style="color:#3b82f6">3. Wellness Economy Explosion</span>

The $4.5T global wellness economy is driving unprecedented demand for health-conscious consumer insights. CPG brands are investing heavily in "better-for-you" products and need data to optimize.

### <span style="color:#a855f7">4. Mobile-First Generation</span>

Gen Z and Millennials expect mobile-first financial tools. Legacy scanner panels and surveys can't reach these demographics. Milo's modern iOS experience is designed for this audience.
