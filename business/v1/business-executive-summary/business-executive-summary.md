### Three-Sentence Summary

**MILO** is an AI-powered iOS application that combines receipt scanning, proprietary health scoring, and spending intelligence into a single platform — creating an entirely new category of "wellness-aware spending analytics." The company generates revenue through a three-pillar model: B2C subscriptions (24%), B2B data monetization (66%), and analytics reports (10%), projecting $9.8M in Year 3 revenue with 71% operating margins. With $500K-$750K in seed funding, MILO targets 100,000 monthly active users and a $78M valuation at exit.
