### AI-Powered Finance Apps (2025 Landscape)

| App | AI Capabilities | Price | Platform |
|-----|----------------|-------|----------|
| **Copilot** | Auto-categorization, NLP queries, behavior nudges | $95/yr | iOS/Mac only |
| **Monarch Money** | 90-95% categorization accuracy, forecasting | $99/yr | All platforms |
| **YNAB** | Limited AI (rule-based) | $109/yr | All platforms |

### Key Trends

```diff
+ AI-native architectures replacing rule-based systems
+ Privacy-first design becoming competitive advantage
+ Household/family finance collaboration features
+ Integration with investment tracking
+ Subscription management as gateway feature
- Declining tolerance for ads in finance apps
- Users willing to pay for premium features
```

MILO enters a market characterized by:

3. **Proven monetization** - Both B2C subscriptions and B2B data have precedent

5. **Technology enabler** - AI cost/performance breakthroughs make vision feasible
