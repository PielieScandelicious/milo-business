## Revenue Pillar 2: Data Monetization

### Market Opportunity
- Global data monetization market: $2.1B (2020) → projected 22.1% CAGR through 2030
- Consumer transaction data market: ~$12B annually
- CPG brands spend billions on consumer panel data
- Receipt data is considered "gold standard" for purchase validation

### The Milo Data Advantage

Milo collects highly valuable data that CPG brands, retailers, and financial institutions desperately need:

- **Item-Level Purchase Data:** Exact products, quantities, prices
- **Store Attribution:** Where consumers shop
- **Category Spending:** 16-category classification
- **Health Scoring:** Nutritional choices tracking
- **Temporal Patterns:** Shopping frequency, seasonality
- **Demographic Correlation:** Via Firebase profiles

### Data Monetization Models

**Model A: Data-as-a-Service (DaaS)**
- Industry benchmark: $50-200 per panelist annually
- Conservative estimate for Milo: $25-75 per consenting user/year
- Key requirement: Explicit user consent and robust anonymization

**Revenue Projection:**

| MAU | Consenting Users (60%) | Annual Revenue |

| 10,000 | 6,000 panelists | $300,000 |
| 50,000 | 30,000 panelists | $1,500,000 |

**Model B: Insights-as-a-Service**
- Monthly CPG category reports: $2,500-5,000/report
- Custom brand tracking dashboards: $10,000-25,000/year
- Real-time trend alerts: $1,000-3,000/month subscription

### Critical Success Factors
1. Privacy compliance (GDPR, CCPA, state laws)
2. User consent management
3. Data anonymization infrastructure
4. Minimum panel size: 10,000+ for statistical relevance
5. Demographic representativeness

## Strategic Recommendations

1. **PRIORITIZE APP GROWTH FIRST** - Data monetization and analytics require scale. Focus on reaching 50,000 MAU before heavily investing in B2B revenue streams.

2. **BUILD PRIVACY-FIRST DATA INFRASTRUCTURE** - Invest early in consent management, anonymization, and compliance. This enables premium data pricing and reduces legal risk.

3. **DEVELOP B2B SALES CAPABILITY** - Hire or partner with experienced B2B data sales professionals. CPG and retail data sales require specialized relationships.

4. **CREATE TIERED DATA ACCESS** - Offer Basic, Professional, and Enterprise data tiers to capture value across customer segments.

5. **ESTABLISH THOUGHT LEADERSHIP** - Publish free trend reports to build credibility and generate B2B leads for premium products.

6. **NEGOTIATE VERYFI VOLUME PRICING** - At scale, OCR costs are significant. Secure volume discounts or explore alternatives.
