## Channel Testing Framework

### New Channel Evaluation

| Criteria | Weight | Threshold |
|:---------|:------:|:----------|
| CAC Potential | 30% | <$15 |
| Scale Potential | 25% | 10K+ installs/year |
| Audience Fit | 20% | Health-conscious 25-45 |
| Effort Required | 15% | Manageable |
| Time to ROI | 10% | <6 months |

