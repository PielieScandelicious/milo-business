## Creative Testing Framework

### Testing Protocol

| Test Type | Variables | Duration | Success Metric |
|:----------|:----------|:---------|:---------------|
| Hook test | 3-4 openers | 5 days | View rate |
| Format test | Video vs. carousel | 7 days | CTR |
| CTA test | "Try free" vs. "Start now" | 5 days | Install rate |
| Audience test | Interest vs. LAL | 14 days | CPA |
