## Review Management

### Review Prompt Strategy

```
╔═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                    REVIEW PROMPT TRIGGERS                                     ║
╠═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                               ║
║   TRIGGER 1: After 5th receipt scanned                                       ║
║   → User has experienced core value                                          ║
║                                                                               ║
║   TRIGGER 2: After viewing first health insight                              ║
║   → Moment of delight                                                        ║
║                                                                               ║
║   TRIGGER 3: After 2 weeks of active use                                     ║
║   → Established habit                                                        ║
║                                                                               ║
║   TRIGGER 4: After upgrading to paid                                         ║
║   → Committed user, likely satisfied                                         ║
║                                                                               ║
║   RULES:                                                                      ║
║   • Max 3 prompts per year per user                                          ║
║   • 30-day minimum between prompts                                           ║
║   • Never prompt during negative experience                                  ║
║                                                                               ║
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

### Review Response Template

| Rating | Response Approach |
|:------:|:------------------|
| 5★ | Thank + highlight feature they mentioned |
| 4★ | Thank + ask what would make it 5★ |
| 3★ | Apologize + offer support |
| 1-2★ | Apologize + direct to support + commit to fix |
