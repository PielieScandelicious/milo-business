## Strategic Implications

### Leverage Strengths

1. **Health Scoring** → Lead marketing with wellness angle
2. **Privacy** → Position as trustworthy alternative
3. **Dual Revenue** → Reduce investor risk perception

### Address Weaknesses

1. **User Base** → Allocate 20% of funding to acquisition
2. **iOS-Only** → Plan Android for Year 2
3. **GDPR** → Prioritize compliance before launch

### Capture Opportunities

1. **Health Trend** → Target Yuka users, fitness influencers
2. **B2B Demand** → Build sales capability early
3. **AI Normalization** → Highlight AI features

### Mitigate Threats

1. **Subscription Fatigue** → 14-day trial, clear value
2. **Big Tech** → Move fast, establish category
3. **CAC Inflation** → Focus on organic, ASO
