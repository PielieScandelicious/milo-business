## Competitive Threat Assessment

### Direct Competitors

| Competitor | Threat Level | Rationale |
|:-----------|:------------:|:----------|
| **Fetch Rewards** | 🟡 Medium | Large user base, could add health features |
| **Ibotta** | 🟡 Medium | Similar OCR capability, different value prop |
| **Receipt Hog** | 🟢 Low | Outdated UX, limited features |

### Indirect Competitors

| Competitor | Threat Level | Rationale |
|:-----------|:------------:|:----------|
| **Copilot** | 🟡 Medium | AI-native, premium positioning |
| **Monarch Money** | 🟡 Medium | Growing fast, good UX |
| **YNAB** | 🟢 Low | Different methodology, no receipts |
| **Yuka** | 🟢 Low | Health only, no spending |

### Potential Entrants

| Threat | Probability | Impact | Response |
|:-------|:-----------:|:------:|:---------|
| **Apple Health+ Finance** | Low | Critical | Niche differentiation |
| **Google Wallet expansion** | Low | High | Privacy positioning |
| **Fetch adds health** | Medium | High | Speed to market |
