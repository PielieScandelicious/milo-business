## Remediation Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4) - €35-50K

```
Week 1:
- Draft privacy policy
- Document legal basis per activity
- Begin DPA outreach to processors

Week 2:
- Privacy policy legal review
- Execute Veryfi DPA
- Execute Google/Firebase DPA

Week 3:
- Execute Railway DPA
- Begin consent UI design
- Define data retention periods

Week 4:
- Deploy consent management system
- Test consent flows
- Documentation review
```

### Phase 2: Rights & Controls (Weeks 5-8) - €25-40K

```
Week 5:
- Build data export function
- Build deletion function
- Design rights portal UI

Week 6:
- Deploy rights portal
- Test SAR workflow
- Document transfer mechanisms

Week 7:
- Execute SCCs with US processors
- Transfer impact assessment
- Update privacy policy

Week 8:
- End-to-end testing
- Staff training (rights)
- Documentation finalization
```

### Phase 3: Governance (Weeks 9-12) - €15-25K

```
Week 9:
- Begin DPIA for AI processing
- Begin DPIA for data monetization

Week 10:
- Complete DPIAs
- Risk mitigation review
- Control implementation

Week 11:
- Appoint DPO (if required)
- Incident response procedures
- Staff training (incidents)

Week 12:
- Compliance audit
- Final documentation
- Launch readiness review
```
