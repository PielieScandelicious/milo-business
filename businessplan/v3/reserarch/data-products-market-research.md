## 1. NielsenIQ Byzzer

**What it is:** A self-service SaaS platform purpose-built for small and emerging CPG brands. Byzzer democratizes access to NielsenIQ's retail measurement and consumer panel data, which was previously only affordable for large enterprises.

### Data Products & Reports

Byzzer offers a library of **15+ report types** across these categories:

| Report Category | Specific Reports | What It Measures |
|---|---|---|
| **Performance** | Category & Brand Trend Report | Weekly sales volume, distribution vs. category benchmarks, market share |
| **Pricing** | Price Comparison vs. Category | Normal and promoted prices vs. key competitors |
| **Promotion** | Promotion Effectiveness | Promotional lifts, price elasticity |
| **Distribution** | Distribution & Velocity | Store-level distribution, sales velocity per point of distribution |
| **Brand Score** | Brand Scorecard | Composite score across Promotion, Pricing, Assortment, and New Products — compared to category |
| **Smart Reports** | AI-powered recommendations | Uses NIQ elasticity models and promotional lifts to provide actionable recommendations |
| **Consumer Panel** | Shopper Demographics & Behavior | Who buys, how often, basket composition, demographic profiles |
| **Data on Demand** | Raw data extracts | Up to 1 million data points for download, parameterized by categories, facts, markets, time periods |

### Pricing

| Package | Price | What's Included |
|---|---|---|
| **Free Tier** | $0 | 3 free report runs to trial the platform |
| **Starter** | ~$4,950/year | 20 report runs, 5 markets, 6 categories |
| **Growth / Startup CPG** | ~$10,000/year | Starter pack (discounted for Startup CPG members), top retailers (Whole Foods etc.), weekly data updates, retail measurement + panel access, 5 team seats, 5 categories |
| **Custom / Enterprise** | Custom pricing | More categories, more markets, additional report runs |

### Delivery Format
- **Self-service web dashboard** with interactive reports
- **Data on Demand**: downloadable CSV/Excel extracts
- **BI alerts**: automated weekly email notifications on brand performance changes

### Key Insight for Milo
Byzzer proves there is a massive SMB market willing to pay $5K-$10K/year for packaged, self-service data products. The reports are templated (not custom) which keeps delivery costs low. The "Brand Score" concept -- a simple composite metric -- is particularly clever as a hook for small brands.

## 2. YouGov / GfK Consumer Panel

**What it is:** YouGov acquired GfK's Consumer Panel Services (CPS) business in 2023 for EUR 315 million. CPS GfK has been running FMCG consumer panels across Europe for 60+ years. This is the dominant consumer panel in continental Europe.

### Scale & Coverage

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Countries | 18 European countries (including Belgium) |
| Households in panel | 107,000+ |
| Purchase acts measured | 18 million+ per year |
| SKUs tracked | 2.1 million |
| Retailers covered | 1,500+ |
| Shopper KPIs available | 85 |

### Data Products & Reports

**Core Panel Products:**

1. **Shopper Insights Subscription** -- Continuous tracking of who buys what, where, how much, how often, and why
   - Penetration (% of households buying)
   - Repeat rate
   - Frequency of purchase
   - Weight of purchase (spend per household)
   - Loyalty metrics
   - Channel switching behavior

2. **Category Diagnostics** -- Deep analysis of category performance by brand, segment, retailer, and channel

3. **Shelf Management & Assortment Optimization** -- Data to support range reviews and planogram decisions

4. **Pricing Strategy Validation** -- Shopper data by channel, brand, and retailer to validate pricing architecture

5. **SimIT Web Platform** (new, developed with Circana) -- Self-service shopper analytics for category management recommendations

6. **FMCG Shopper Types** -- An internationally harmonized segmentation model blending real purchase data with shopper attitudes and opinions. The only segmentation that unifies purchase behavior + attitudinal data.

7. **Brand Health Tracking** -- Daily tracking across 16 core brand health metrics with competitive benchmarking

8. **Consumer Segment Activation** -- Audience segments derived from panel data that can be activated in media buying

### Delivery Format
- **SaaS platform** (24/7 access via web-based analytics tool)
- **4-weekly or monthly reporting cadence** (panel data requires aggregation for robustness)
- **Custom analyses** by in-house expert teams
- **Exportable data** for client BI tools

### Pricing
- Not publicly disclosed. Enterprise contracts typically run **EUR 50K-500K+/year** depending on number of categories, countries, and depth of access.
- Likely tiered by number of categories and markets subscribed.

### Key Insight for Milo
YouGov/GfK is the dominant FMCG panel in Belgium. Their 85 shopper KPIs represent the "gold standard" metrics that brands expect. Milo should aim to replicate the most-valued subset of these KPIs. The segmentation product (blending purchase data + attitudes) is a premium differentiator that Milo could replicate by combining receipt data with in-app surveys.

## 3. Circana (formerly IRI)

**What it is:** Circana was formed from the merger of IRI and The NPD Group. They are one of the two global giants (alongside NielsenIQ) in retail measurement and consumer panel data. Their Liquid Data platform is their flagship technology.

### Platform Architecture: Liquid Data

Liquid Data is a cloud-based, end-to-end analytics platform that integrates:
- Point-of-sale (POS) data
- Consumer panel data
- Loyalty card / frequent shopper data
- Hundreds of third-party data sets
- Proprietary client data

Deployable on Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud, AWS, and Oracle Cloud (also hybrid/private).

### Product Lineup

| Product | Target | What It Does |
|---|---|---|
| **Liquid Data Go** | SMBs & emerging brands | Turnkey, affordable self-service analytics. 20 curated personalized reports delivered in 48 hours. POS data, panel behavior, e-commerce trends, pricing across key retailers. |
| **Liquid Data Collaborate** | Mid-market to enterprise | Full analytics platform for sales, share, distribution, pricing, promotions across categories |
| **Liquid Data Engage** | Retailers | Customer-centric strategies using loyalty and panel data |
| **Liquid Supply Chain** | SMBs | Entry-point into supply chain planning using real-time data |
| **Liquid Testing** | All sizes | Market testing and modeling tool |
| **Complete Consumer** | Enterprise | Blended receipt, loyalty card, scan panel, and survey data into a unified shopper view |

### Liquid Data Go -- The SMB Product (Direct Competitor Model for Milo)

Key features:
- Instant access to syndicated POS data
- Monitor sales, share, distribution, pricing, and promotions
- 20 curated and personalized reports in 48 hours
- Built-in training and guided support (no data expertise needed)
- Coverage: beauty, club, dollar, mass, grocery, online pure play
- ROI calculator available on their website

### Pricing
- **Liquid Data Go**: Not publicly disclosed but positioned as "affordable" for SMBs. Industry estimates suggest **$5K-$15K/year** depending on category and market scope.
- **Enterprise Liquid Data**: Custom pricing, typically **$100K-$1M+/year**.

### Delivery Format
- **Self-service web platform** with interactive dashboards
- **In-app training** and guided workflows
- **Data exports** available
- **API access** for enterprise clients

### Key Insight for Milo
Circana's "20 curated reports in 48 hours" for Liquid Data Go is a powerful template for how to package panel data for SMBs. The concept of pre-built, personalized reports that require zero data expertise from the user is exactly what small Belgian food brands need.

## 6. European Grocery Data Startups

### 6A. Foxintelligence (acquired by NielsenIQ)

**What it was:** The largest e-commerce consumer panel in Western Europe. 600,000-person panel across France, Germany, Italy, Spain, and UK.

**How it works:** Gathers and anonymizes e-receipts (email receipts) in real time to create transaction data. Measures e-commerce market shares category-by-category, region-by-region.

**Products:**
- Real-time e-commerce market share tracking
- Consumer journey and consumption habit analysis
- Competitive purchase analysis
- B2B SaaS platform for data access

**Status:** Acquired by NielsenIQ. Now integrated as "Digital Purchases by NIQ" and feeds into NIQ's Omnisales solution for Western Europe.

**Key Insight for Milo:** Foxintelligence proved the model of receipt-based panel data in Europe and was acquired for significant value. Their focus was e-receipts (email); Milo's physical receipt scanning covers a different (and arguably richer) data source.

**Tier 1 -- Launch Products (require smallest panel size):**

### 8.3 Competitive Positioning

**Milo's unique advantages vs. incumbents:**

1. **Multi-retailer view** -- Unlike Colruyt Insights or Delhaize Enlight+ which only show their own stores, Milo captures purchases across ALL Belgian retailers.
2. **SMB-accessible pricing** -- NielsenIQ/Circana/YouGov are too expensive for most Belgian food SMBs. Milo can serve the EUR 1K-10K/year market.
3. **Belgium-specific** -- Global players lack granularity in small markets like Belgium. Milo can be THE Belgian grocery data specialist.
4. **Consumer-permissioned** -- First-party, GDPR-compliant, consent-based data (unlike modeled or inferred data from other sources).
5. **Agility and speed** -- Near real-time data vs. 4-week reporting cycles of traditional panels.
6. **Receipt + survey hybrid** -- Ability to combine purchase data with attitudinal data via in-app surveys (like YouGov's FMCG Shopper Types but at lower cost).
