## Panel Scale & Capabilities
- **45,000 weekly active panelists** in Spain
- **~25,000 daily shopping baskets**
- Captures roughly **1 in 400 grocery receipts** in Spain

- Data updated **4 days** after month/quarter-end (vs. 7 weeks for traditional panels)
- Covers **full shopping baskets**, store-level pricing, promotions, purchase missions

## PROMOS App Mechanics (Firsthand Testing)
- User browses available "promos" (category-level: "Leche" €0.03, "Cerveza" €0.03, etc.)
- User selects promos they claim to have bought
- User scans receipt
- AI validates if promo item is on receipt (24-48h)
- **NO base reward per receipt** — only paid for validated promos
- Rewards shrinking over time: from €0.10 to €0.01 per promo
- **Trustpilot rating: 1.6/5** — complaints about rejections, tiny payouts, opaque process
- Play Store claim "Cada ticket te da cashback" is misleading marketing

## Key Weaknesses of PROMOS/Algori Model
1. **No base receipt reward** → incomplete data capture (users skip when no interesting promos)
2. **Shrinking rewards** → trust erosion (€0.10 → €0.01)
3. **No standalone app utility** → zero reason to use beyond cashback
4. **No gamification** → purely transactional, no emotional stickiness
5. **Opaque rejection UX** → users wait days, promos silently rejected
6. **Receipt-only product matching** → no external shelf/promo data enrichment
7. **Promo-gated model** → biased toward promo-buyers, misses full baskets

## Milo Competitive Advantages vs PROMOS/Algori

### Data Quality
- **Digital receipts only** (pre-structured text) vs camera photos (OCR errors)
- **Daltix EAN matching** → exact product identification, Nutri-Score, shelf prices
- **Cross-retailer promo tracking** via Daltix → "was this on promo when bought?"
- **price_gap_pct** metric (actual paid vs shelf price) — Algori cannot do this
- **Spend-based rewards** → captures ALL receipts, not just promo-relevant ones

### Consumer App
- **Guaranteed base cashback** (0.5-1.5%) vs no base reward
- **4-tier gamification** (Bronze→Diamond) with spins, streaks, jackpots
- **5 retention moats**: wallet, streaks, badges, purchase history, AI context
- **Standalone utility**: spending analytics, health scoring, AI chat, deals engine
- **Belgian-specific**: 13 stores, 3 languages, local taxonomy

### B2B Product
- **Per-category pricing** (€149-799/mo) vs custom enterprise deals only
- **SMB-accessible**: 98.5% cheaper than YouGov
- **22 SKU metrics + 15 brand switching metrics** in structured data products
- **Excel/CSV delivery** → clients pivot/filter themselves

## Market Validation (What Algori Proves for Milo)
